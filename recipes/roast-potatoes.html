Roast Potatoes

Description

Make Perfectly Roasted Potatoes at Home With/ Without Oven

Roasted potatoes are basically well cooked/baked potatoes which have a crispy outside and a fluffy inside. Roasted potatoes are normally served as a side dish and you can serve them with butter, cheese or sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Rosemary

Steps
  1. First, wash the potatoes with skin on or you can peel the skin off if you like. Cut them into halves or you can cut them into quarters if you’re using bigger potatoes
  2. Boil water in a pan. Add 1 tbsp salt to the boiling water
  3. Now parboil the potatoes in salted water for about 10 minutes
  4. Prepare the infused oil in a small pan by adding the minced garlic and rosemary to the olive oil and fry them until slightly brown
  5. Strain the infused oil to the parboiled potatoes
  6. Then add a little salt, black pepper, and the chili powder
  7. Mix everything until well combined
  8. Then shake the potatoes well to rough up the edges. Roughing up edges creates an uneven surface which helps to make an extra crunchy outer layer
  9. Place half of the potatoes in a baking tray lined with parchment paper and bake them into a 200℃ oven for about an hour flipping them halfway through
  10. You can bake them all in the oven or if you have less time you can also pan roast the potatoes, however, way you prefer
  11. To pan roast, the potatoes place a heavy bottomed frying pan or a skillet on medium heat, add some of the infused oil to the pan and place the rest of the potatoes in the pan
  12. Then fry them for 15-20 minutes until they get crispy, flipping them every 5 minutes
  13. Garnish the potatoes with some freshly chopped coriander
  14. They are perfectly crunchy outside and creamy/fluffy inside. Enjoy!
